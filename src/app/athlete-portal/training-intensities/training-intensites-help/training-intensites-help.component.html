Training Intensities

Your training intensities (or zones) are calculated based on your swim, bike, and run performance assessments.

These intensities will change based on your environment.  The training intensities shown on the Training Intensities page and your Training Schedule by default are based on your current environment using TriDot's E-Norm Technology.  You can edit the T.H.E.N parameters shown on the Training Intensities panel to calculate appropriate training intensities for a specific environment.
